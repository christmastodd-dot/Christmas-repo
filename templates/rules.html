How It Works

  • Participants draft 5 state legislators from a pool of 51
  • Each legislator is exclusive to one team
  • The admin awards points based on legislator actions during session
  • All scoring events are visible in the Activity Feed
  • Team and individual legislator rankings are tracked on the Leaderboard
