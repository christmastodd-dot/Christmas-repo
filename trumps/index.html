How to Play Trumps

Overview

Trumps is a 4-player card game played in partnerships (N/S vs E/W). You are South, partnered with North. Win packs (rounds of 4 cards) to score. First team to 3 wins takes the game.

The Deal

52 cards, no jokers. Each player gets 12 cards. 4 cards go to the kitty.

Bidding

Starting left of the dealer, players bid 3-7 (packs above 6 your team will win). Choose High (ace is best) or Low (ace is best low). Low outranks High at the same number. First bidder must bid. Pass if you can't or won't bid higher.

Kitty & Trump

The bid winner picks up the 4 kitty cards, discards 4, then declares the trump suit. If you were first bidder and won, you can choose direction and trump after seeing the kitty.

Playing Packs

Bid winner leads first. You must follow suit if you can. If you can't, play any card (including trump). Highest card in the led suit wins, unless trumped. In Low, the lowest card wins.

Scoring

If the bidding team takes enough packs (6 + bid), they win the hand. Otherwise, the defending team wins. If the defense clinches early, the hand ends immediately. First to 3 hand wins takes the game.

Bolos

BOLOS — bidding team wins all 12 packs! SIDE BOLOS — bidding team wins 11 of 12. REVERSE BOLOS — defense clinches without the bidding team winning a single pack.
